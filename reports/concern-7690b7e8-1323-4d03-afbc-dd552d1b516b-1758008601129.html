Strategy 2: Strategic Peer Partnering

  • Implementation Steps:

1. Partner Selection (5 mins): Identify a supportive peer and briefly explain their role is to be a "helper," not to do the work for Josie.
2. Task Launch (2 mins): Give the pair very specific instructions. "Josie and Sam, your job is to list three causes of the event. Sam, you will talk and write. Josie, your job is to find the evidence in the text and show Sam."
3. Teacher Circulate: Monitor the partnership, modeling how to explain and include.
  • Data Collection: Observe the quality of peer interaction and Josie's engagement during partner work.

  • Success Criteria: Successful completion of a collaborative task with both partners engaged.
