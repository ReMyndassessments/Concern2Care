1. Day 3 (Pre-Assess): Before starting a new chapter, give Abby the chapter test or a 5-question pre-assessment of the core skills.

  • Data Collection: Record scores on pre-assessments and document the alternative assignment provided.

  • Success Criteria: Successful completion of compacting contracts and high-quality work on extension projects.
